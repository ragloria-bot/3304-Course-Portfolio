Discussion 03: Presenting Your Design

Link to my Figma Design: Figma Design Plate Mate is a mobile and web application designed to simplify meal management by offering a personalized experience. Users input their preferences, dietary restrictions, and culinary skills, allowing the app to suggest tailored recipes, meal plans, and grocery lists. The app also features a food discovery section, "Browse Recipes," that encourages users to explore new recipes, cooking techniques, and global culinary trends, transforming meal preparation into an enjoyable activity.

Core Problem and Solution:

Problem:

Many face challenges with preparing healthy meals at home, including:

  • 1. Time-consuming meal planning: Finding new, healthy recipes can be burdensome. 2. Lack of inspiration: Repetitive meals reduce motivation to cook. 3. Fragmented tools: Managing multiple apps for recipes and grocery lists is inefficient.

Solution:

Plate Mate integrates these functions into a single platform, providing personalized meal plans and a dynamic recipe library tailored to user preferences and dietary needs.

User Flow:

  • 1. Sign-In/Account Creation: New users can create an account by entering their full name, email address, and password. If you already have an account, simply sign in. 2. Account Holder Preferences: After signing up, new users will set up their profile. They need to specify dietary preferences (like vegetarian or gluten-free), list any allergies, and choose favorite cuisines. Users will also select their cooking experience level, preferred cuisines, and meal plan preferences for certain meal times. Donâ€™t forget to indicate how many people you are cooking for, as the recipes will adjust accordingly. 3. Navigation Menu: Upon preferences submission, users will see a navigation menu to access their personalized "Meal Plan," which includes two recipe options for each meal. They can also create a "Grocery List," adjust their "Meal Preferences," or "Browse Recipes" beyond their personalized meal plan. 4. Meal Plan: By clicking the "Meal Plan" button, users go to their personalized 7-day meal plan with two recipe options for every meal. 5. Ingredients/Instructions: Once a recipe is chosen, the user will receive a list of ingredients and cooking instructions. 6. Grocery List: Users can access their "Grocery List" anytime to add items they need. 7. Navigation Buttons: At the top and bottom of each screen, the user is provided with options to navigate throughout the app with ease.

Justification of a Specific Design Decision:

Design Decision: A clear, easy-to-use search and filtering system is included in the "Navigation Menu". Justification: This decision meets a key user need for efficiency and personalization.
