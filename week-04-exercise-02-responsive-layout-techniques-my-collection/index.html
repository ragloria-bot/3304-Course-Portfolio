Places I Have Traveled

This is a glimpse into the many places I have explored with my husband and daughter, each filled with treasured memories that we hold dear. From the sun-kissed shores of breathtaking beaches where we built sandcastles and splashed in the waves, to the enchanting atmosphere of a famous theme park where we laughed on thrilling rides, every destination tells a unique and special story for our family. This collection of photos not only captures the stunning beauty of the great outdoors whether it is the vibrant colors of a sunset over the ocean or the majestic peaks of a mountain range but also reflects the joy and love we have shared during our adventures together. Each image is a snapshot of a moment that reinforces our bond, reminding us of the laughter, excitement, and connection we feel while exploring the wonders of nature as a family. Together, we create memories that we will cherish for years to come.

Grand Cayman Islands

Travel Destination | 2017 | Family Vacation

The Grand Cayman Islands are one of the most memorable places my family and I have visited together. The beaches boast stunning crystal-blue waters and powdery white sand, creating a picturesque paradise. The weather consistently hovers around a perfect 80 degrees, complemented by a gentle, refreshing breeze that makes outdoor activities enjoyable. During our stay, we also embarked on a thrilling stingray tour in the open ocean. At first, I was apprehensive about encountering these beautiful creatures, but it quickly turned into one of the highlights of our vacation. The stingrays glided gracefully through the water, and I was amazed at how large they were. Their skin felt soft and slippery as I gently touched them, making the experience both surreal and unforgettable.

Disney World

Theme Park | 2010 | Family Vacation | Orlando

Disney World had always been a cherished dream of mine. Growing up in Florida, I longed to experience its magic but never had the chance due to financial struggles. When I became a parent, I wanted to give my daughter those special memories. For her 5th birthday, I planned a 7-day trip to Disney World, staying at the beautiful Polynesian Resort, surrounded by lush gardens and tropical plants. Each day was filled with excitement as we explored the parks and met beloved characters. My daughter's eyes sparkled with joy, and every evening she would say, "Mom, this is the best day ever!" This trip was a wonderful opportunity to bond with her and finally share the magic I had always dreamed of.

Key West

Island | 2018 | Family Vacation | Florida Keys

Key West, Florida, is a captivating place with a distinct charm. The locals are warm and welcoming, eager to make visitors feel at home. While the beaches might not be the best, the island's stunning landscapes and vibrant sunsets compensate for it. As you stroll through the quaint streets, you'll find local art galleries, charming cafes, and shops that highlight the island’s rich culture. Experiencing Key West and its natural beauty was truly a memorable journey.

Freeport Bahamas

Island | 2018 | Family Vacation | Freeport Bahamas

Freeport was a fascinating destination with its primary industry centered around oil storage, blending, and transshipment. The port area is dominated by an array of industrial equipment, which gives the surroundings a distinctly urban feel, somewhat overshadowing the natural beauty I had anticipated. Despite this industrial backdrop, what truly stood out to me were the locals. The people in Freeport were not only welcoming but also exuded a genuine humility that made my experience memorable. Their warmth and friendliness created a stark contrast to the heavy machinery around them, revealing a vibrant community that thrives amidst the industrial landscape. Overall, my time in Freeport offered a unique combination of industrial insight and heartfelt human connection.

Cozumel Mexico

Island | 2019 | Family Vacation | Cozumel Mexico

Mexico never ceases to amaze, and our trip to Isla Pasión, a breathtaking private island, was no exception. After disembarking and walking down to the beach, we were greeted by tiny hermit crabs, much to my daughter's delight as she loves all creatures. The beach features crystal-clear waters that invite you to swim, and we enjoyed some of the best fish tacos we've ever tasted, made with fres local ingredients. The stunning scenery and delicious food made for an unforgettable experience on this enchanting island.

Montego Bay Jamaica

Island | 2017 | Family Vacation | Montego Bay Jamaica

One of my favorite memories from Montego Bay, Jamaica, has to be rafting down the Martha Brae River. I loved soaking in the scenic views and the fresh air while our guide effortlessly navigated the raft. Another highlight was relaxing on Doctor's Cave Beach, where the crystal-clear waters were simply irresistible. I also enjoyed exploring the vibrant atmosphere of the Hip Strip, and the catamaran cruise we took was unforgettable, especially with that fun stop at Margaritaville. Each moment made my time there truly special!

Moon Cay Bahamas

Island | 2019 | Family Vacation | Moon Cay Bahamas

Private islands provide a unique experience that everyone should embrace at least once in their lives. The allure goes beyond just the stunning tropical beaches; it lies in the chance to disconnect from the hustle and bustle of daily life and immerse oneself in nature. During this vacation, I enjoyed an incredible array of activities, including swimming in crystal-clear waters, snorkeling among vibrant coral reefs, and observing the diverse marine life that the ocean has to offer. The tranquility of the surroundings, combined with the beauty of colorful fish darting around me, created a truly memorable and rejuvenating escape.

Oahu Hawaii

Island | 2021 | Family Vacation | Oahu Hawaii

When COVID hit, it impacted every household like a ton of bricks, so why not travel? There were many tests and lots of paperwork required to travel to Oahu, Hawaii, but it was totally worth it! Our timing was perfect; Hawaii had been locked down for a year, but the week we arrived, all the shops and restaurants had just reopened. The locals were thrilled to finally be able to breathe freely again and see other visitors. The local cuisine is amazing, and the beautiful landscapes seem to go on for miles. When COVID hit, it felt like a heavy weight on every household, but we decided to embrace the possibility of travel and set our sights on Oahu, Hawaii. Despite navigating tests and paperwork, the adventure proved worth it. Our timing was perfect; just as we arrived, Hawaii lifted its lockdown and shops and restaurants began reopening. The locals welcomed us with excitement, eager to reconnect with visitors after nearly a year of restrictions. Exploring Oahu was a delight, with its vibrant local cuisine offering everything from fresh poke to delicious plate lunches. The stunning landscapes, from golden beaches to dramatic cliffs, took our breath away. Each moment felt like a celebration of the island's beauty and spirit, reminding us of the joy that travel can bring.
